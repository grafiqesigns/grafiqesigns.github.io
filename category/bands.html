Designing

As we are into Signage field, we help our clients develop the solution design – the concept, business case and content model for a project. We help them understand their objectives and how signage displays can help realize them. We help with resource allocation and budgeting, and we ensure the right technology is designated for the right places – so that the screens make a clear, measureable difference.

Printing

The state of the art in- house facilities at Colors Digital include designing, scanning, printing, lamination, mounting and installation. We work upon vast variety of medias like vinyls, posters, flex, translites, mesh, cloth, fine art canvas and many innovative medias to cater our customer’s specific requirements. Our printing house includes high end H.P inkjet printers for high resolution printing quality for in shop branding along with eco solvent printers for cost effective printing.

Fabrication

Fabrication is the building of structures by cutting, bending, and assembling processes .is the act of taking raw stock material and turning it into a part for use in an assembly process. There are many different types of fabrication processes with different materials like M.S, S.S, Brass, Acrylic, Aluminum etc.
