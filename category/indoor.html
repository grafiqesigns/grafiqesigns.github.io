Designing

As we are into Signage field, we help our clients develop the solution design – the concept, business case and content model for a project. Printing

The state of the art in- house facilities at Colors Digital include designing, scanning, printing, lamination, mounting and installation. We work upon vast variety of medias like vinyls, posters, flex, translites, mesh, cloth, fine art canvas and many innovative medias to cater our customer’s specific requirements. Our printing house includes high end H.P inkjet printers for high resolution printing quality for in shop branding along with eco solvent printers for cost effective printing.
