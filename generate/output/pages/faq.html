Faq

FAQ

  • Home
  • Frequently asked questions

1. Why do I need a creative design agency?

Ans: Simple, if you have a business then you need a brand Identity and positioning and in today’s fast moving world, image is everything. We have a team of professionals working on strategies to make your company stand out and say the right thing.

2. What will be my responsibility as a client?

Ans: Number one will be timely communication. Effective and clear client communication determines project timeline and anything related to project deadline. And, it’s always good when client makes timely payments. 

3. What is creative brief and how important it is?

Ans: Any design job is based on creative brief, which will come from your end. The brief is a starting point and not the answer. More clear and elaborate the brief is then more creative and relative the outcome will be.

4. Why do I need signage and wayfinding consultancy?

Ans: Visit to a new environment is an unfamiliar experience. The communication provided by signage and way finding contributes significantly to the visitors overall impression of the environment. We at Grafiqe signs do a detailed study of surroundings and design way finding strategies, which help visitors to determine the best route to quickly and easily navigate to their destination.

5. Can clients give logo in jpeg format?

Ans: No, the high resolution formats like cdr. (Corel Draw) or ai. (Adobe Illustrator) formats are accepted.

6. What is your usual turnaround time?

Ans: Turnaround time depends on the complexity of the project. Once we have creative brief and all necessary approvals from you end, we usually take 4 to 5 business days to deliver initial design.

7. What sort of credit time can be given to clients?

Ans: Normally no credit time will be given to clients. Company has the strict policy of 50% advance and remaining 50% at the time of delivery/installation.

8. How do I pronounce your name?

Ans: It’s pronounced “GRAFIQE SIGNS”….and YES; we went a little bit too creative with that, the combination of creativity in signages.


In case you haven't found the answer for your question please feel free to contact us, our customer support will be happy to help you.
